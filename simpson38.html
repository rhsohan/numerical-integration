Simpson's 3/8 Rule Formula

Definite integration is approximated by fitting cubic polynomials through groups of four adjacent points:

$$I = \frac{3h}{8} \left[ y_0 + y_n + 3\sum_{i \neq 3k} y_i + 2\sum_{i = 3k} y_i \right]$$
